Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Deep dive into AI/ML technologies, neural networks, and practical applications

Date: December 15, 2024
Time: 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM
Hall: Main Hall A
Duration: 2 hours
Level: Intermediate
Format: Presentation + Q&A
Register Now Add to Schedule

Session Description

This comprehensive session explores the latest developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning. You'll learn about neural network architectures, training methodologies, and real-world applications across various industries.

What You'll Learn:

  • Understanding neural network architectures and deep learning fundamentals
  • Best practices for training and optimizing ML models
  • Real-world applications of AI in business and technology
  • Ethical considerations in AI development
  • Future trends and emerging technologies in the field

Who Should Attend:

This session is ideal for developers, data scientists, product managers, and anyone interested in understanding how AI and ML can be applied to solve real-world problems. Some basic knowledge of programming and statistics is recommended.

Speaker Profile

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Chief AI Officer, TechCorp

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a leading expert in artificial intelligence and machine learning with over 15 years of experience in the field. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from MIT and has published over 50 research papers on neural networks and deep learning.

Currently serving as the Chief AI Officer at TechCorp, Dr. Johnson leads a team of 50+ researchers and engineers working on cutting-edge AI solutions. She has been instrumental in developing AI systems that have transformed industries ranging from healthcare to finance.

Expertise: Neural Networks, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP
