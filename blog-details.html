Early Bird Tickets Now Available

Published: October 15, 2024 | Author: Conference Team

We're excited to announce that early bird tickets for Global Tech Summit 2025 are now on sale! This is your chance to secure your spot at the year's premier technology conference at a special discounted price.

Early Bird Pricing

For a limited time, you can save up to $100 on your conference ticket. Early bird pricing is available until November 30th, 2024, or until tickets sell out. Don't wait - these tickets are going fast!

What's Included

Your early bird ticket includes:

  • Access to all conference sessions and keynotes
  • Workshop participation (subject to availability)
  • Networking events and receptions
  • Conference materials and swag bag
  • Lunch and refreshments for all three days
  • Access to the conference app and digital resources

Ticket Tiers

We offer three ticket tiers to suit different needs:

  • Early Bird: $299 (Regular: $399) - Perfect for individual attendees
  • Standard: $399 (Regular: $499) - Includes workshop access
  • VIP: $599 (Regular: $699) - Premium experience with exclusive benefits

Group Discounts

Bringing a team? We offer special group discounts for 5 or more attendees. Contact our sales team at tickets@techsummit2024.com to learn more about group pricing.

How to Purchase

Purchasing your ticket is easy:

  1. Visit our tickets page
  2. Select your preferred ticket tier
  3. Complete the registration form
  4. Make your payment securely online
  5. Receive your confirmation email with all the details

We can't wait to see you at Global Tech Summit 2025! This promises to be our biggest and best conference yet, with over 50 speakers, 100+ sessions, and 2,000+ attendees from around the world.
